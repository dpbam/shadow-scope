At Scope Now

Are you at the Shadow Scope right now? Here's how to use it!

To start the Shadow Scope, double-click with your finger on the program button and then hit the green arrow button.


Now, you need to locate the slide to put your sample in:

Then, you can use the pipette to collect a sample! In this example, we will be looking at Daphnia magna, but you can look at pond water, sea monkeys, moss, or any other tiny organisms you would like!


Collect the organism(s) you want to look at inside a pipette and then carefully transfer them into the reservoir of the sample holder:


Now, insert the sample holder into the scope:

Great! Now you can move around the slide until you see your organism! Once you see an organism in the screen, hit the green button to record a 10-second video

Now, the screen will show you the unique code and/or timestamp associated with your video, so you can find it later on our website!
